Introduction

This is a digital supplementary to my thesis 'Reconstruction from Spatio-Spectrally Coded Multispectral Light Fields' supervised and reviewed by Michael Heizmann and co-reviewed by Bastian Goldl√ºcke. The thesis concludes my work as a research associate with the Institute of Industrial Information Technology at Karlsruhe Institue of Technology from 2017 to 2022. Supplementing the written thesis, this website contains links to an additional, interactive evaluation of the proposed reconstruction approaches using a Binder notebook. Here, all investigated methods can be assesed in terms of their test dataset performance but also visualize their inference using different synthetic and real-world datasets. In addition to the results presented in this thesis, further scenes and evaluation metrics can also be evaluated. Furthermore, you'll find links to my public repositories for the implementaion of all considered methods and conducted experiments.
